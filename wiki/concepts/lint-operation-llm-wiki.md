# Lint Operation in LLM Wiki

## Overview

The lint operation is a periodic health check for the LLM Wiki to ensure consistency, accuracy, and completeness.

## How It Works

The LLM reviews the wiki to identify contradictions, outdated information, orphan pages, missing cross-references, and data gaps. It suggests new questions or sources to address these issues, keeping the wiki accurate and well-maintained.

## Key Properties

- **Error Detection:** Identifies contradictions, outdated claims, and missing links.
- **Proactive Maintenance:** Suggests new questions and sources to improve the wiki.
- **Consistency:** Ensures the wiki remains coherent and up-to-date as it grows.

## Practical Applications

Used to maintain the health and accuracy of a growing knowledge base.
