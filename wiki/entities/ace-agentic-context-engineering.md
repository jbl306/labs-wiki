# ACE (Agentic Context Engineering)

## Overview

ACE is a framework for evolving and optimizing contexts in large language model (LLM) applications. It treats contexts as comprehensive, structured playbooks, incrementally updated and refined through modular workflows. ACE addresses the limitations of brevity bias and context collapse, enabling scalable, self-improving LLM systems.

## Relevance

ACE demonstrates significant performance gains and efficiency improvements in both agent and domain-specific benchmarks, outperforming strong baselines and enabling self-improving LLMs without labeled supervision. Its modular, incremental approach is increasingly practical with advances in long-context LLMs and context-efficient inference.
