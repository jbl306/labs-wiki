# AdaBoost

## Overview

AdaBoost (Adaptive Boosting) is an ensemble learning algorithm that combines multiple weak classifiers, typically decision stumps, to form a strong classifier. It works by sequentially training classifiers and adjusting sample weights to focus on misclassified instances.
