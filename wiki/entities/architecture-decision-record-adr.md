# Architecture Decision Record (ADR)

## Overview

An Architecture Decision Record (ADR) is a structured document that captures significant architectural decisions, their rationale, alternatives considered, and the context in which they were made.

## Relevance

ADRs are a key tactical component of the Living Documentation Principle, providing authoritative context for both agents and humans when encountering legacy patterns or conventions.
