# AutoAgent

## Overview

AutoAgent is an open-source, fully-automated LLM agent framework that enables users to build, customize, and deploy agents and workflows using natural language alone. It supports multi-agent orchestration, self-managing workflows, and integrates with a wide range of LLM providers, offering both CLI and containerized deployment options.

## Relevance

AutoAgent democratizes AI agent development by eliminating the need for manual coding, allowing rapid prototyping and deployment of agentic systems. It ranks highly on benchmarks like GAIA and offers flexible integration with LLMs, making it a leading platform for research, business automation, and educational applications.
