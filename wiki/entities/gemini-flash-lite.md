# Gemini-flash-lite

## Overview

Gemini-flash-lite is a lightweight, cost-efficient language model in Google's Gemini lineup, used for noise filtering, structural re-ranking, and synthesis in the Proxy-Pointer pipeline. It enables semantic detection and ranking without expensive LLM calls.
