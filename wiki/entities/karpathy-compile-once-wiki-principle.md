# Karpathy Compile-Once Wiki Principle

## Overview

Karpathy's compile-once principle is a methodology for knowledge management that emphasizes compiling answers once and maintaining them as durable artifacts. In agentic wikis, this principle guides the design of workflows where agent-generated answers are filed back as persistent pages, rather than being recomputed for each query.

## Relevance
