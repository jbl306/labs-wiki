# LexisNexis (Lexis+ AI)

## Overview

LexisNexis is a leading legal technology provider offering Lexis+ AI, an AI-driven legal research tool that employs retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to answer legal queries. The tool is marketed as 'hallucination-free,' claiming to deliver accurate and authoritative answers grounded in a closed universe of legal content.
