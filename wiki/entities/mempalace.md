# MemPalace

## Overview

MemPalace is an open-source, local-first AI memory system designed to organize and retrieve conversational and project data with high fidelity. It employs a palace-inspired hierarchical structure and achieves state-of-the-art recall on the LongMemEval benchmark by storing raw verbatim exchanges and leveraging semantic search. The system is adaptable, runs entirely on the user's machine, and integrates with popular AI tools via MCP.

## Relevance

MemPalace addresses the problem of ephemeral AI conversations by preserving every decision, debugging session, and debate in a structured, navigable memory. Its architecture yields significant retrieval improvements and supports both solo and team workflows, making it a leading solution for persistent AI memory.
