# RLM-Qwen3-8B

## Overview

RLM-Qwen3-8B is the first natively recursive language model, created by fine-tuning Qwen3-8B to operate as a Recursive Language Model (RLM) with recursive sub-calls. It demonstrates substantial performance improvements on long-context tasks, approaching the quality of vanilla GPT-5 in several benchmarks.

## Relevance

RLM-Qwen3-8B validates the effectiveness of native recursion in LLMs, outperforming its base model by 28.3% on average and enabling dense access to long prompts.
