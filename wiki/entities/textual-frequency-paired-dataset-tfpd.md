# Textual Frequency Paired Dataset (TFPD)

## Overview

The Textual Frequency Paired Dataset (TFPD) is a curated benchmark introduced in this paper to evaluate the impact of sentence-level frequency on LLM performance. It consists of pairs of paraphrases—one high-frequency, one low-frequency—for each original sentence, with human annotation ensuring semantic equivalence.

## Relevance

TFPD enables controlled experiments on the effect of textual frequency in prompting and fine-tuning LLMs, supporting the empirical validation of TFL, TFD, and CTFT.
