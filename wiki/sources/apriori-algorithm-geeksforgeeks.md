# Apriori Algorithm - GeeksforGeeks

## Summary

This article explains the Apriori Algorithm, a foundational technique for association rule mining in data analysis. It details the step-by-step process of identifying frequent itemsets, generating candidate groups, pruning infrequent sets, and producing association rules using metrics like support, confidence, and lift. Practical applications and a grocery store example illustrate its use in real-world scenarios such as e-commerce and recommendation systems.

## Key Points

- Apriori Algorithm identifies frequent itemsets and association rules in transactional datasets.
- Uses support, confidence, and lift metrics to evaluate the strength of item relationships.
- Widely applied in market basket analysis, product recommendations, and customer behavior analysis.

## Concepts Extracted

- **[[Apriori Algorithm]]** — The Apriori Algorithm is a classic method for discovering frequent itemsets and generating association rules in transactional datasets. It is central to market basket analysis and is widely used for uncovering patterns of co-occurrence among items, enabling businesses to optimize recommendations and product placement.
- **Association Rule Mining Metrics** — Support, confidence, and lift are key metrics used to evaluate the strength and significance of association rules generated by the Apriori Algorithm. These metrics help determine which item relationships are meaningful and actionable in transactional datasets.
